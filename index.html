S.M.A.R.T. Cleanse

Surface Management, Automated Response and Total Disinfection Cleanse System

Introduction

Maintaining high standards in disinfection and hygiene in workplaces, learning facilities, and healthcare settings is a significant challenge in the new normal. With the continuous evolution of infectious pathogens, there is a growing necessity for an efficient, smart, and effective way to disinfect and monitor shared spaces in order to protect employees, students, patients, and the community itself from the spread of infectious diseases. One of the shared spaces that have the highest susceptibility of bacterial and viral contamination are the surfaces in restroom cubicles. Traditional disinfecting methods via human intervention are often inadequate, time-consuming, and resource-intensive, making it imperative to find a solution that can address these concerns comprehensively and sustainably. This problem calls for a product that can provide automated, real-time disinfection and monitoring in a way that is adaptable to different cubicle profiles and aligns with the “new normal” emphasis on safety and well-being as well as complying with the advent of renewable, sustainable energy

SMART Cleanse is an innovative monitoring and disinfecting device designed to address the heightened hygiene and safety requirements in workplaces, learning facilities, and healthcare settings. It offers a comprehensive solution that combines automated disinfection with real-time monitoring and reporting, ensuring the cleanliness and safety of shared spaces.

SMART Cleanse is a versatile, solar-powered, wireless, and scalable device that combines disinfection methods with real-time monitoring and SMS notifications. It addresses the critical need for enhanced hygiene and safety in various settings, from workplaces and educational institutions to healthcare facilities, aligning with the requirements of the "new normal" and contributing to sustainable development goals related to health and clean energy.

The device was initially conceived and designed using AutoCAD, where the parts and proposed design were developed, including the initial dimensions. Over time, the design was refined to its final appearance. This evolution from the initial CAD design to the final product reflects a meticulous design process.

The device is designed for seamless interconnectivity with other components through separate microcontrollers for each cubicle, offering cost-effective scalability and ease of installation, especially for mass production. Furthermore, it features a unique power management system that can operate via solar energy or a hybrid mode. In the event of a power interruption, the device can seamlessly switch to solar energy, ensuring uninterrupted operation. Additionally, the device employs a two-pronged disinfection approach using UV light and disinfecting liquid sanitation to guarantee thorough disinfection in each cubicle during every use. These innovative features are integral to the device's functionality and efficiency.

The SMART Cleanse device holds significant societal importance in today's "new normal," marked by heightened health consciousness and the need for improved hygiene practices. It plays a pivotal role in infection prevention by effectively disinfecting surfaces in various settings, reducing the risk of disease transmission and safeguarding public health. Moreover, the device promotes health equity by ensuring equal access to sanitized environments across workplaces and educational institutions, fostering inclusivity in healthcare. Users report increased confidence and trust in their surroundings, positively impacting mental well-being. Additionally, SMART Cleanse contributes to community protection, particularly for vulnerable populations, and alleviates the burden on healthcare systems by reducing infection transmission, supporting healthcare professionals in delivering essential care more efficiently.

Conclusion

SMART Cleanse use of solar power for its operation is a highly innovative feature. This self-sustaining power source allows it to function independently, reducing its carbon footprint and ensuring continuous operation even during power outages. This innovation aligns with sustainable development goals (SDGs), particularly SDG 7, which focuses on clean and affordable energy access for all.

The device's wireless capabilities and modular design make it adaptable to a wide range of environments. The ease with which it can be assembled and installed in various cubicle profiles is another innovative aspect that enhances its versatility.

SMART Cleanse has an ability to provide real-time monitoring and SMS notifications to maintenance crews when disinfection liquid is low. This proactive approach ensures that the reservoirs are always filled and repetitive checking action on the maintenance crew is alleviated.

The use of dual disinfection methods, including disinfecting liquid misting and surface UV light disinfection, is a unique and innovative approach. This combination ensures comprehensive and effective sanitation of surfaces, making it more reliable in the fight against pathogens. The innovation supports SDG 6, which emphasizes the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation.

SMART Cleanse unlimited scalability is a feature that sets it apart. Whether used in a small office or a large healthcare facility, the device can adapt to any number of cubicles. This scalability caters to the diverse needs of various settings and efficiency as well

The real-time display of disinfection progress as a percentage is an original feature. It provides visual feedback and reassurance to users that the device is effectively sanitizing surfaces, enhancing transparency and trust in the disinfection process.

In conclusion, SMART Cleanse disinfection capabilities hold significant social importance by preventing infections, promoting health equity, instilling confidence, protecting communities, and reducing the burden on healthcare systems. It was also proven that the design of the SMART Cleanse was widely accepted by the community. The modularity and simplicity of the installation was ingenious and novel. The interviews conducted with users highlight its positive impact on individuals and society as a whole, particularly in the context of enhanced hygiene practices during the "new normal."
